# Privacy Policy (draft)

> Working draft — review with a qualified UK adviser before launch. Aligned to
> UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025 and
> PECR.

## Who we are

PT Management is the data controller for the personal data described here.
Contact: _to be completed_.

## What we collect

- **Account:** name, email, phone, language preference, hashed password.
- **Health & fitness data (special category):** notes your trainer records to
  plan your sessions. Only collected and used with your **explicit consent**.
- **Bookings & payments:** appointments, package credits, payment records
  (amounts and Stripe identifiers — we never store card numbers).
- **Cookies:** essential cookies always; analytics only with consent (PECR).

## Why and on what basis

- Run your account and bookings — **contract**.
- Process health data for session planning — **explicit consent** (Art. 9(2)(a)).
- Marketing and analytics — **consent**, withdrawable at any time.

## Who we share it with (processors)

- **Stripe** — payment processing.
- **Railway** — hosting and database.
- **Google (Gemini)** — AI features, on minimised/de-identified data.

## How long we keep it

See the Data Retention Schedule (`data-retention.md`).

## Your rights (UK GDPR)

Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and
withdrawing consent. Use the **Privacy page** in your account to download your
data, manage consents, or request erasure. You can also complain to the ICO
(ico.org.uk).

## Security

Passwords are hashed with argon2id; we apply security headers and least-
privilege access. Access to health data is restricted and audit-logged.
