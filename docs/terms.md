# Terms of Service (draft)

> Working draft — must be reviewed by a qualified UK solicitor before launch.
> Plain-English summary of the key terms a client agrees to at registration.

## 1. The service

PT Management lets clients book personal-training sessions with their trainer
and pay online. A client account is linked to a single trainer.

## 2. Bookings

Sessions are booked into specific hourly time slots offered by the trainer.

## 3. Cancellation policy (important)

You may cancel or reschedule a session **free of charge up to 24 hours before**
its start time (UK time, Europe/London).

**Cancellations made within 24 hours of the start time, and missed sessions
(no-shows), are charged in full.** This applies whether you paid up front, used
a session from a package, or pay by Direct Debit.

This is a contract for a service to be supplied on a specific date/time. Under
the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges)
Regulations 2013, the 14-day cancellation right does not apply to such
leisure-activity bookings. The 24-hour policy above governs cancellations.

## 4. Payment

You can pay by:
- **Card, up front** for a single session.
- **Package** (e.g. a block of sessions) purchased in advance as credits.
- **Direct Debit (Bacs)** — see the Direct Debit Guarantee below.

Card details are handled by Stripe; we never see or store your full card number.

## 5. Direct Debit Guarantee (Bacs)

- The Guarantee is offered by all banks and building societies that accept
  instructions to pay Direct Debits.
- If the amount or date of a payment changes, we will tell you **in advance**
  (normally at least the agreed notice period) before your account is debited.
- If an error is made by us or your bank in the payment of a Direct Debit, you
  are entitled to a full and immediate refund from your bank.
- You can cancel a Direct Debit at any time by contacting your bank; please also
  tell us.

## 6. Your data

See the Privacy Policy. You can manage consents and exercise your data rights
on the Privacy page in your account.
